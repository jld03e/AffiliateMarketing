About Affiliate Marketing Template Pages

AMTP aims to provide all of the necessary tools you need to use or customize the look and feel of webpages that connect customers to your products.

To facilitate this, we provide our users the source code needed to style and even create their own pages on our platform.

We've even included a snazzy 'how to' portion to the site with ways to read through the instructions and use our templates or apply the changes you want to our templates!


THEMES


Light Theme

The Light Theme emphasizes product descriptions and media for your product. Includes an action button to conenct the user to the point of sale to complete the transaction.

Dark Theme

The Dark Theme emphasizes product pricepoints by displaying price information more prominently in the description of the item, and even before the description. Includes same access to media as the Light Theme.

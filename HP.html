About Affiliate Marketing Template Pages

AMTP aims to provide all of the necessary tools you need to use or customize the look and feel of webpages that connect customers to your products.

To facilitate this, we provide our users the source code needed to style and even create their own pages on our platform.


THEMES


Light Theme

The Light Theme emphasizes product images and a light color pallete. Includes an action button to connect the user to the point of sale to complete the transaction.

Dark Theme

The Dark Theme emphasizes a darker color pallete, along with videos related to your products. Includes same access to media as the Light Theme.
